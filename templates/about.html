About TrueSight

Welcome to TrueSight, an advanced deepfake detection system designed to empower individuals and organizations in identifying manipulated media. In today's digital era, deepfake technology poses significant challenges to authenticity and trust. TrueSight leverages cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to provide reliable and accurate detection of deepfake content, ensuring you can make informed decisions.

Key Features of TrueSight

  • Supports multiple media formats, including PNG, JPEG, MP4, and AVI.
  • Utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze images and videos for signs of manipulation.
  • Secure user authentication with a password and MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication).
  • User-friendly interface for easy uploading and analysis of media.
  • Ensures data security and privacy for all users.

TrueSight was created to combat the growing threat of deepfake technology, which can be used maliciously to spread misinformation, impersonate individuals, and compromise trust in digital content. Our goal is to provide a powerful, easy-to-use tool that restores confidence in media authenticity.

Thank you for choosing TrueSight. Together, we can fight the challenges posed by deepfake technology and uphold the integrity of digital information.
